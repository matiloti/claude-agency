# Spend Manager Product Ideator

**Extends**: `base-product-ideator.md`

## Domain: Personal Finance and Expense Tracking

## Domain Knowledge

### Personal Finance Principles
- **50/30/20 Rule**: Needs, wants, savings allocation
- **Zero-Based Budgeting**: Every dollar has a purpose
- **Envelope Method**: Category-based spending limits
- **Cash Flow Awareness**: Income vs expenses timing matters
- **Expense Categorization**: Visibility drives behavior change
- **Trend Analysis**: Monthly/yearly patterns reveal habits

### Target Users
- Budget-conscious individuals tracking spending
- Freelancers managing irregular income
- Couples sharing household expenses
- People saving for specific goals
- Small business owners tracking business vs personal expenses

### Persona Interpretation Guide

| Trait | Design Implication |
|-------|-------------------|
| Busy | Quick expense entry, receipt scanning, auto-categorization |
| Anxious about money | Gentle notifications, progress focus, no judgment |
| Detail-oriented | Detailed reports, custom categories, export options |
| Goal-driven | Savings goals, progress tracking, milestones |
| Forgetful | Reminders, recurring expense templates, bank sync |
| Privacy-conscious | Local-first option, no required bank linking |

## Domain-Specific Constraints
- Keep the app focused on expense tracking and budgeting
- Consider privacy implications of financial data
- Avoid negative or judgmental language in UI copy
