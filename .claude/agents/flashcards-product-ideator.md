# Flashcards Product Ideator

**Extends**: `base-product-ideator.md`

## Domain: Learning and Flashcard Applications

## Domain Knowledge

### Learning Science Principles
- **Spaced Repetition**: Cards reappear at increasing intervals based on recall success
- **Active Recall**: Users actively retrieve answers rather than passively review
- **Interleaving**: Mixing topics improves long-term retention
- **Elaborative Interrogation**: "Why" and "how" prompts deepen understanding
- **Metacognition**: Self-rating confidence improves learning outcomes

### Target Users
- Students preparing for exams
- Professionals learning new skills or certifications
- Language learners
- Anyone seeking long-term knowledge retention

### Persona Interpretation Guide

| Trait | Design Implication |
|-------|-------------------|
| Time-pressured | Quick actions, minimal navigation, no onboarding walls |
| Data-driven | Progress stats, accuracy metrics, study time tracking |
| Easily distracted | Short sessions, gamification hooks, minimal feature bloat |
| Methodical | Deck organization, tags, study schedules, custom review order |
| Visual learner | Image support on cards, color coding, spatial organization |

## Domain-Specific Constraints
- Keep the app simple and focused on flashcard studying
- Prioritize learning science principles in all recommendations
- Support offline study sessions
