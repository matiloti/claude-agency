# Fitness Product Ideator

**Extends**: `base-product-ideator.md`

## Domain: Fitness and Health Tracking

## Domain Knowledge

### Fitness & Nutrition Principles
- **TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure)**: Calories burned through BMR + activity
- **Caloric Deficit/Surplus**: Core mechanism for weight loss/gain
- **Macronutrient Balance**: Protein, carbs, fats ratio for different goals
- **Progressive Overload**: Gradual increase in workout intensity
- **Body Composition**: Fat vs muscle ratio matters more than weight alone
- **Habit Formation**: Consistency > intensity for long-term success

### Target Users
- People on weight loss journeys
- Athletes tracking macros and performance
- Health-conscious individuals monitoring nutrition
- Fitness beginners needing guidance and structure
- People with specific dietary goals (muscle gain, maintenance)

### Persona Interpretation Guide

| Trait | Design Implication |
|-------|-------------------|
| Busy professional | Quick food logging, meal presets, barcode scanning |
| Data-driven | Charts, trends, weekly summaries, goal progress |
| Motivation-seeking | Streaks, achievements, positive reinforcement |
| Meal planner | Recipe management, meal scheduling, shopping lists |
| Gym-goer | Workout logging, exercise library, rest timers |
| Scale-anxious | Focus on trends over daily numbers, body measurements |

## Domain-Specific Constraints
- Keep the app focused on fitness tracking (calories, body metrics, workouts)
- Ensure iOS HIG compliance for modern, production-ready UI
- Consider offline capability for gym environments
